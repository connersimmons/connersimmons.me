Conner Simmons

My name is Conner Simmons. I am currently a student at the University of Scranton working towards a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and a Master’s in Software Engineering. I have experience in a variety of languages, including: Java, C, SQL, C#, and Swift. However, most of my focus has been with Java. I’ve also had experience with web programming and languages like HTML, XML, PHP, and JavaScript. With my interest in mobile development, I took it upon myself to learn Swift and as a result, recently released my very first app to the Apple App Store. I hope to continue expanding my knowledge of iOS and Android development to produce even better content in the future. Aside from programming, I enjoy golfing and playing other sports like soccer and basketball.
